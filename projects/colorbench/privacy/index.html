Privacy Policy.

Last updated 2026-04-24 · Version 1.0 · The Mini Colorbench

TL;DR

We don't collect any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device. No data leaves your phone.

Who we are

The Mini Colorbench ("the app") is developed and operated by:

Philipp Buchwald (trading as Pixel Paladin)
Contact: hi@pixel-paladin.de
Website: pixel-paladin.de

What the app does

The Mini Colorbench is a companion app for miniature painters. It stores palettes, paint collections, hobby projects, and notes locally on your device. We do not have a backend server. Your data never leaves your phone unless you explicitly export it.

Data we collect

1. Nothing about you

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, address, IP address, location, or any other identifying information.
  • We do not require an account.
  • The app does not track your behavior (no analytics, no ads, no fingerprinting, no advertising ID).
  • The app does not sell or share any data, because it has no data to share.

2. Crash diagnostics

v1.0 collects nothing at all. A future version may add anonymous crash diagnostics; we'll update this policy before that ships.

3. Google Play Store

When you install the app from Google Play, Google collects standard install telemetry under Google's Privacy Policy. We do not control this and we do not see your individual install/uninstall data — only aggregated counts.

Data we do not collect

  • Analytics about feature usage
  • Advertising data
  • Device contacts, calendar, photos, or files (the app does not request these permissions)
  • Microphone, camera, or location (the app does not request these permissions)

Your rights under GDPR

As an EU/EEA user, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of any data we have about you. (In practice: we have none — v1.0 doesn't collect anything.)
  • Deletion — request deletion of any data. (Same caveat — there's nothing to delete.)
  • Object / Opt-out — not applicable; no data is collected.
  • Lodge a complaint — with your national data protection authority. The German supervisory authority is the BfDI.

Children

The app is rated for general audiences (PEGI 3 / IARC Everyone). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Because we do not collect data from anyone, this is automatic.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will change the "Last updated" date at the top and note the change in the app's About screen. Material changes will be announced in the app's release notes on Google Play.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hi@pixel-paladin.de.
